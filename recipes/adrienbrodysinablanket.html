Adrien Brodys in a Blanket

Description

Word on the street is that Adrien Brody is quite vain and likes to surround himself with admirers. Who better to admire Adrien than Adrien himself?

You guessed it, Adrien Brody invested his Hollywood riches in a cloning machine to make sure that he is always surrounded by people who love him.

To date, Adrien has cloned himself eleven times.

Ingredients

  • 11 Adrien Brodys
  • Other ingredients

Directions

  1. Purchase a large mirror (this is important)
  2. Locate the Brody posse on a sunny day (likely at a white tie event or foreign film festival or something similarly posh)
  3. Approach the posse, signaling admiration
  4. Prop up the mirror in front of the gang of Brodys, reflecting the sun's very strong rays
    • They will not be able to look away from their own image, blinding themselves in the process
  5. Judo chop each Brody, in turn.
  6. Prepare everything else etc.
  7. Put the Adrien Brodys in the blankets

Congratulations you have made Adrien Brodys in a Blanket
